I designed the infographic below using Canva, as it seemed the easiest option for me at the time. While I am by no means a skilled infographic designer, Canva’s pre-made templates and design tools were very helpful for a novice like myself. While it took a little while to get the spacing and colors right, overall, it was a manageable task, and surprisingly, quite interesting.

The infographic includes the following topics: password security, safe internet usage, email etiquette, safe use of social media, and cybercrime awareness. The information is presented in a simple manner, ensuring that the reader can understand the points clearly, without going into too much detail. Instead, the infographic focuses on some of the safe practices we might be getting into the habit of doing, like reusing passwords or clicking on random links without thinking twice.

While creating the infographic, I noticed how we, as users, access the internet daily but do not necessarily practice safe internet usage at all times. It was a very enlightening experience, as I also learned how to present information in a simple manner, without going into too much detail, as opposed to lengthy descriptions.
